## ⚠️ Two same-name decoys — do NOT use

Ship names get reused, and the wrong one is always the easiest to find:

- **HMS *Suffolk* (1941)** — the County-class cruiser that shadowed the *Bismarck* in the Denmark Strait.
  **A different ship entirely.** Arthur's *Suffolk* is the **1903 Monmouth-class armoured cruiser**.
- **HMS *Audacious* (1912)** — the King George V-class battleship photographed 1913–14 (LoC 17766).
  Not the 1869 ironclad. *(Logged here too because it bit once already — see the Henry Whitehead folder.)*

**Caption note:** the *Suffolk* photograph is dated **1919**, four years after Arthur left her. Same ship,
later date — the caption in the story says so plainly rather than implying he is in the picture.
