## Still wanted

**A picture of HMS *Audacious* (1869)** — the ironclad that relieved *Endymion* on the Humber and on
whose books Henry finished his service.

⚠️ **BEWARE THE DECOY.** The obvious Commons hit, `File:HMS Audacious LOC 17766.jpg` (Library of
Congress, Bain collection), is the **WRONG SHIP** — it is the **King George V-class battleship
HMS *Audacious* of 1912**, photographed 1913–14, *forty-three years too late*. Do not use it.
Same name, different vessel. Look instead in Commons under **Audacious-class ironclad**, or use a
sister ship (**HMS *Invincible*** 1870, **HMS *Iron Duke*** 1870, **HMS *Vanguard*** 1870) — but if a
sister is used, **the caption must say so plainly**.
